Solar energy is becoming increasingly popular in Algeria. With an average of over 3000 hours of sunshine per year, our country has exceptional solar potential.

## Types of Solar Panels

### Monocrystalline Panels
Monocrystalline panels are the most efficient (18-22% efficiency). They are ideal for roofs with limited space.

### Polycrystalline Panels
Slightly less efficient (15-18%), but more affordable. Great value for large surfaces.

## Selection Criteria

1. **Power**: Expressed in watt-peak (Wp), it determines electricity production.
2. **Warranty**: Check product warranty (10-12 years) and performance warranty (25 years).
3. **Certification**: Ensure panels are CE and IEC certified.

## Installation in Algeria

Installation must be done by a certified professional. Sizing depends on your electricity consumption and available roof space.
